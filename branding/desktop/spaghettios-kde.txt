# SpaghettiOS Plasma Theme Notes

## Colors
- Background: #1a0000 (Deep dark red)
- Foreground: #F5DEB3 (Wheat/cream)
- Accent: #FFD700 (Gold)
- Selection: #8B0000 (Dark red)

## Theme locations
- Plasma theme: /usr/share/plasma/desktoptheme/spaghettios
- Color scheme: /usr/share/color-schemes/SpaghettiOS.colors
- Icon theme: /usr/share/icons/SpaghettiOS
- Cursor theme: /usr/share/icons/SpaghettiOS-Cursors
- Aurorae theme: /usr/share/aurorae/themes/SpaghettiOS
- SDDM theme: /usr/share/sddm/themes/SpaghettiOS

## Recommended icon sets
Base on Papirus or Tela icons, recolored with the SpaghettiOS palette.
